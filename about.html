About me

Hi! I'm Beatriz, I'm from Portugal and I have a degree in Computer Engineering. I have always shown interest in Illustration, Photography and Design in my free time. At this moment I want to explore my more creative side combined with the knowledge I acquired in Computer Science.

Studies
